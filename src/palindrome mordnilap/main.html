Intro

So for my 2020 mothers day card, I decided to go with a bold white text on a background of different messages in pink text on a slightly lighter background. One of the many words in the background was “madam”, a nickname for my mum I realized was a palindrome. Now I knew of palindromes, words that are spelled the same forwards and backward, but I didn’t realize how many exist in the words I use. The other thing that struck me is when coupled with a geometric font, the symmetry of palindromes really shines and you can make some interesting designs out of them.

So using the excellent font Mont, I made a dozen designs out of palindromes and made the graphic you can find bellow. I realize that some words are easy to identify while others not, so you can click the word to find it in the footnotes along with some of my musings.
